Corruptopoly Instructions
Yippee, through the funding of your political party, you managed to create a SUPER PAC. Unfortunately, you will have to compete with other PACs to gain influence and bankrupt them

also playable on mobile!


Basically
  1. Player 1 is Clinton, Player 2 is Bush, Player 3 is Obama, Player 4 is Trump
  2. Buy seats in the house of representatives
  3. Use pork-barrel spending to buy utilities
  4. Collect donations from your competitors when they land on your districts
  5. Find information about spaces by putting your mouse over them(if it only shows a cost, then that space is unowned)
  6. The fee for landing on a utility is the number on the dice times the multiplier(10 if both utilities are owned by the same person, 4 otherwise)
  7. You might be sent to federal prison for illegal campaign finance methods, but you get pardoned if you roll doubles
  8. If you land on chance or community chest, the SCOTUS moves you a random number of spaces
  9. Upgrade your representatives to raise more money
  10. You get $200 for landing on or passing go
  11. Franking privilege(free parking)/Amtrak(railroad) don't do anything unfortunately
  12. If you run out of money you lose
  13. Any bugs represent problems with the government
